Stratego

Behind every success is a plan. We meet, we talk, we give & take and formulate your needs, wants and goals to direct your business.

Identity

Who are you? Your identity is centered on the logo and its color scheme. A useful and recognizable logo is simple and should seek a balance of both bold and subtle.

Design

You’ll need a cohesive look in everything you produce and offer as a business. I design unique, useable and inviting websites - along with many other deliverables extending to print, ads, motion and promotional items.

Campaigns

Promote your services & values through several media. Querycreative makes it happen with experience in designing and creating marketing materials and copy.

Production

‘Everything-ography’ on demand – I handle creating and editing photos, videos, and animations. When you need expert advice, I provide consultations and brainstorming on your next marketing and identity leap.

Development

With extensive experience and a thirst for continued improvement and learning, Querycreative provides you with not only the latest, but the most future-proof of structures for your site.

Device-Agnostic

With over half of web traffic flowing through mobile devices and Google taking a firm stance on boosting mobile-friendly sites, it is necessary to have a site that is flexible in and accommodating of various sizes.

CMS

With Ruby on Rails, you’re in control - a custom, robust and secure admin is built to suit your needs. I also have vast experience developing on Wordpress and will gladly tailor to your preference.

SEO

SEO is built right in from start to finish. I’ll even get you started on Google Analytics for monitoring and tracking of information on how your site is performing.

Social Media

A site alone is but an island, Social Media provides the bridge to the mainland - I ensure your site and media are visually cohesive to promote your business and establish an online rapport with potential clients.

Process

Creativity is knowing how to hide your sources

Ideation

Exploration and research is initiated based on project guidelines – this includes brainstorming and developing core concepts and themes for the project.

Inspiration

Creation of sketches and design mock-ups in Adobe Creative Suite programs leads to connecting concepts with the visual elements of design on a fundamental level.

Implementation

Reiteration and sometimes consternation, leads to the final phase. After approval, development occurs – whether coding, printing or simply sending off the deliverables, we’ve reached the destination.
