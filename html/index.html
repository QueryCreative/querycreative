What I Do

I allow my passion for both slick User Interfaces and smooth User Experiences to direct my work while leveraging my plethora of capabilities (required for any developer to be successful) which include the following: a strong grasp of CSS/SASS, HTML/5 (HAML/SLIM), ECMAScript: JS/jQuery/CoffeeScript, Ruby (specifically Ruby on Rails), WordPress and robust Git experience.

Not only do I develop on the front-end and dabble in the back-end, but I also have a decade of experience heavily utilizing the Adobe Creative Suite's array of applications, specifically Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Dreamweaver (give me Sublime anyday) and After Effects. This expansive and over-lapping knowledge set allows me to utterly conquer any challenge in a web design, web development or UI/UX role.

With a total of eight years and counting of experience designing, coding and rocking websites, I’ve become steeped in the most useful and robust coding languages and frameworks while also garnering significant design and development experience to mold content into an elegant experience for web users.

As a bastion of web design standards, responsive design, and UI/UX principles, I strive to bring slick, slender and sexy websites to life through determination, inspiration and perspiration.
